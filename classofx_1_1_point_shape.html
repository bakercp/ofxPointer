Detailed Description

A PointShape describes the shape of a pointer.

For standard pointers, such as a mouse or a pen, the width and height of the PointShape will be set to zero while some touch pointers may define the size of the touch or even an ellipse describing the size and angle of a finger tip.

When the ellipse is defined, the width and height should describe and axis- aligned bounding box of the ellipse.
